Fugio is free for you to use but it took one guy a huge amount of time, hard work, and money to make it.

If you like this project, why not show a bit of support?

It doesn't just mean giving money (although you can do that too!), you can:

  • Tell your friends about it!
  • Like the Facebook Page
  • Post up on Twitter and Instagram with #fugio
  • Subscribe to the blog
  • Report all bugs
  • Help document on the wiki
  • Fix bugs via GitHub
  • Invite Alex to give talks, do interviews, run workshops (he's based in the UK)
  • Lend/Give Hardware and Software to see them supported in Fugio
